    Our Services for you.

    At The Summerhouse Cafe & Restobar, we aim to offer a diverse range of services to cater to different preferences and occasions. From dining experiences to creative workshops and event hosting, our goal is to provide a memorable and dynamic destination for our guests.

    01

    Dine In or Dine Out, Day to Night:

    Afternoon Retreat: Enjoy a leisurely lunch or afternoon tea in our welcoming and comfortable atmosphere. Whether you're catching up with friends or having a business meeting, we offer a diverse menu to suit all tastes.


    Nighttime Revelry: As the sun sets, The Summerhouse transforms into a vibrant restobar. Indulge in a curated dinner menu and crafted cocktails while soaking in the lively atmosphere. Our venue is the perfect setting for a night out with friends or a special celebration.


    02

    Rent the Place

    Host your private events, celebrations, and gatherings in our versatile space. The Summerhouse Cafe & Restobar is available for rent, offering a unique and stylish setting for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events, and more. Our team is dedicated to ensuring your event is seamless and memorable.

    03

    Painting Workshops

    Art and Creativity: Unleash your artistic side with our painting workshops. Join us for a creative and relaxing experience where you can express yourself through art. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced artist, our workshops are designed to inspire and bring out your inner creativity.

    04

    Additional Services

    Dynamic Performances: Immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of live entertainment. Enjoy acoustic sets, live bands, and DJ performances that complement the lively atmosphere of our restobar. Check our event calendar for upcoming performances.


    Community Engagement: Be part of our community engagement initiatives. Experience themed nights that bring people together, and stay tuned for collaborations with local artists and businesses. The Summerhouse Cafe & Restobar is not just a venue; it's a space where connections are forged.

    The Summerhouse Cafe

    Location: 2F Gloriana Bella Building, South Poblacion, Valencia, Negros Oriental.

    In 2008, our culinary journey began, giving rise to The Summerhouse Cafe & Restobar—a dynamic establishment at the intersection of gastronomy and vibrant nightlife. From our roots to the heartbeat of our owner's family, our venue holds a special place, once owned by our grandmother and now transformed into a multifaceted space where culinary excellence meets spirited revelry.

    The Summerhouse Cafe & Restobar is a celebration of family, tradition, and the evolution of taste. What started as a restaurant has blossomed into a haven for those seeking both delectable dining experiences and vibrant nightlife escapades.

    Beyond culinary delights and crafted cocktails, The Summerhouse Cafe & Restobar offers breathtaking views of the plaza and its surroundings. It's a backdrop that has seen countless gatherings, celebrations, and the forging of unforgettable connections. From the captivating vista to the inviting interiors, our venue is an invitation to embrace the convergence of past, present, and the possibilities of the future.
